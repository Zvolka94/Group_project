Delivery


When we accept orders for flower delivery:

Acceptance of orders on the website - around the clock.

Acceptance of orders in online chat, Viber, Telegram, WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram - Mon-Fri from 08:30 to 20:00, Sat-Sun from 09:00 to 20:00.


Delivery time:

If you place an order with delivery, it must be placed 1.5-2 hours before the time when it should be delivered to the recipient.

Delivery can be ordered both to your own address and to the address of the person to whom you want to give a gift.
